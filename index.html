🎮 About Slope

Slope is a fast-paced endless runner where you control a ball rolling down a steep 3D slope. The objective is simple — avoid falling off the edges and dodge the red obstacles for as long as possible. The longer you survive the faster it gets!

⌨️ How to Play

← → Arrow keys to steer left and right
A / D Alternative steering controls

💡 Tips & Tricks

Stay in the middle of the slope as much as possible. Look ahead — not at the ball. React early to turns before they happen. The edges are your biggest enemy so always give yourself room to maneuver.
